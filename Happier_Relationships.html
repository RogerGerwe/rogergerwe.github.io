Couple Therapy Models We Use

Solution Focused Brief Therapy
 You will benefit from our use solution focused therapy.  This model helps you find your desired outcome. It can help you move from a negative view of  your partner toward a positive or healthier solution. As a Solution focused therapist we help you envision a desirable future, and then plan and achieve this future using small and large changes toward your goals. We support and encourage your small and large successes and encourage you to build on your strengths while discouraging focus on their problems.

Marriage & Couple Therapy           We will help your Marriage change and improve your particular problematic communication patterns, and help you repair harmful and negative behaviors that have become dysfunctional while increasing positive interactions for a healthier and happier life together. 

My favorite therapeutic influence is a marriage researcher, John Gottman.  



Family Systems Therapy                 We use the techniques of Family Systems therapy to view your  problems within context of your family instead of behavior of a particular family member. We look specifically at the dynamics of your  family to try to understand it's functioning and problems. We view your family members as part of a complex system with your own patterns, language, rules, roles, beliefs and particular needs. When needed, we help you understand how your childhood family operated, the impact of that role on you, your family rules and expectations, and how that experience has shaped your role in your current family and the way it function.
